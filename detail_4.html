Google Lays off 5000 Employees in Major AI Restructuring

📅 Published 2 Hours Ago⏱️ 3 min readBy Admin

In a shocking move, Google announced layoffs of 5000 employees today. The company says it wants to focus 100% on AI.

The affected departments are Ads, Cloud, and Hardware. Meanwhile, Google is hiring 2000 new AI researchers.

CEO Sundar Pichai said "AI is the future. We must move fast to stay ahead of competitors like OpenAI and Meta."

After the news, Google stock jumped 4%. Experts say this is just the beginning of AI driven job changes.
