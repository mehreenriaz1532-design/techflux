Cybersecurity Updates: Protect Yourself in 2026

📅 Published 2 Days Ago⏱️ 5 min readBy Admin

AI powered hackers are the new threat in 2026. They can now create phishing emails that look 100% real.

New Threat 1: Deepfake Voice Calls. Hackers clone your boss's voice and ask for money.

New Threat 2: AI Malware. Virus that changes its code to avoid antivirus.

How to Protect: Use 2-Factor Authentication, Strong Passwords, and Never click unknown links.
